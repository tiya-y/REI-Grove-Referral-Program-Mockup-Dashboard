Refer & Earn

Share REI Grove, earn cash rewards for every paid member you bring in.

How It Works
1
Copy your link
Every member gets a unique referral link. Share it with fellow investors, landlords, or anyone building their portfolio.
2
They join a paid plan
When your referral completes their $1 one-week trial and makes their first full payment, you earn a one-time reward — $5 if they chose monthly, $75 if they chose annual. That's it, one payment per referral.
3
Cash out at $50
Your rewards build up in your balance. Once you hit $50, we'll automatically send your payout on the 1st of the month. No expiration, no action needed.

Frequently Asked Questions
Payouts happen automatically on the 1st of each month when your balance is $50 or more. We'll send payment via your preferred method (Venmo, PayPal, or check) — no action needed on your end.
Your referral qualifies only after they complete the $1 one-week trial and pay their first full subscription term — either $20/month or $200/year. Starting a trial alone does not earn you a reward. The reward is credited at the moment they make that first full payment.
If they cancel during or at the end of the $1 trial without paying a full subscription term, no reward is earned. Your referral must complete the trial and pay their first full term ($20/month or $200/year) before any reward is credited to your account.
